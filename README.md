## How to Play

1. Click numbers to cross them out in each colored row
2. Numbers must be crossed from left to right (no skipping!)

### Scoring

- 1 cross = 1 point
- 2 crosses = 3 points
- 3 crosses = 6 points
- 4 crosses = 10 points
- 5 crosses = 15 points
- 6 crosses = 21 points
- 7 crosses = 28 points
- 8 crosses = 36 points
- 9 crosses = 45 points
- 10 crosses = 55 points
- 11 crosses = 66 points
- 12 crosses = 78 points

Plus 1 bonus point for locking a row.

Penalties: -5 points each (up to 4)

### Colors

- **Red**: 2-12 (ascending)
- **Yellow**: 2-12 (ascending)
- **Green**: 12-2 (descending)
- **Blue**: 12-2 (descending)
